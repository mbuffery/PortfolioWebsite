Student Support Ticket - Android App

Android Studio, Firebase(noSQL)

This was a group project which included 3 people. It was designed to help the communcation between the teacher and the student. We found at uni, it took too long for the teacher to efficently help students while having a large class size. We designed an app where students can create a support ticket saying their name, computer number and session name. This allowed the teacher to see who is next with an issue by seeing a list of tickets created, We decided to not describe the problem as that would take too long and the student would probably have to repeat the problem when they arrive.
